Sim Cards

Sim Cards are small chips that when inserted into your mobile phone allow make and receive texts and calls, as well as use data.

Without a Sim Card none of these actions are possible. Once always physical, now embedded digital cards built into the phone are available, known as eSIMs.

Phone Insurance

Phone Insurance is a policy that covers the cost of repairing or replacing your phone if it becomes damaged, lost or even stolen.

The majority of phone insurance claims are linked to cracked or falty screens, which if insured can be replaced at no extra charge.

Though not necessary, phone insurance is highly recommended in case of uncertain events.

Most phone manufacturer's offer their own insurance plans to customers.
