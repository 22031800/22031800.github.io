iPhone

From its first model in 2007, the iPhone has gone on to become the world’s most popular and commonly used mobile, with over 3 billion having been sold as of 2025. Designed by Apple, their most popular product is known for its smooth design and intuitive user interface, as well as its compatibility with various other Apple devices.

Apple also boast having the most diverse add ons and accessories avialable for the iPhone compared to it's compeitors, cementing them as the industry leaders in the mobile phone space.

Samsung

Known for their diverse lineup of models and extensive customization, Samsung’s Android devices offer much more freedom and choice than other phone types.

Other advantages Samsung phones posses is the high multitasking capabilities, and industry leading security.

Samsung also offer superior GPU performance compared to competitors, which makes a significant difference when it comes to mobile gaming.

Google Pixel

As the name suggests, Google Pixels are built on Google’s operating system, which provides seamless integration with many Google services.

Modern Pixels also boast a 48mp wide and 50mps telephoto lenses, which makes it a dream for amatuer and proffesional photographers alike.
